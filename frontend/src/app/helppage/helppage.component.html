Transactions

Transactions are the core of our application. You can send and receive transactions with ease.

To send a transaction, simply navigate to the "Send" section, enter the recipient's address and the amount, and hit "Send".

To receive a transaction, share your email with the sender.

Account management

Account management is essential for maintaining your profile and settings.

You can update your profile information, change your password, and delete your account.

You can also save your credit cards info so it's easier and faster adding money to your account.

To access account management, click on your name on the toolbar and choose "Settings".

Credit Cards

Credit cards are a convenient way to add money to your account. You can save your credit card information for faster transactions.

When managing your balance account, you can choose whether to use "Stripe" or "Zap" bank. This last option uses the previous mentioned credit card system. So make sure to follow this instructions before using this system.

To add a credit card, navigate to the "Credit Cards" section on "Settings" and click "Add Credit Card". Enter your card details and save.

Favorites

Favorites allow you to quickly access your most frequent contacts.

To add a contact to your favorites, navigate to the "Favorite" section, then "Add favorite" and select the email of your contact.

To remove a contact from your favorites, simply click the heart icon from the list of favorites.
